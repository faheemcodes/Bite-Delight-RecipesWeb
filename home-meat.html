Cooking Tips for Meat

  • Always let meat rest after cooking for better flavor and juiciness.
  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure proper doneness.
  • Marinate meats for at least 30 minutes to enhance flavors.
  • Cook at the right temperature to avoid toughness.
