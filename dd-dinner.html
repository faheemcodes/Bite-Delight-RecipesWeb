Dinner Tips

  • Plan a balanced meal with protein, vegetables, and healthy carbs.
  • Use fresh herbs and spices to enhance flavors without adding extra calories.
  • Prep ingredients ahead of time for a quicker and stress-free dinner.
  • Try different cooking methods like grilling, steaming, or roasting for healthier meals.
  • Serve with a light side dish or salad to complete your meal.
