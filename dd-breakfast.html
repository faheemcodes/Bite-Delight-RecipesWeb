Breakfast Tips

  • Start your day with a protein-rich breakfast to stay full longer.
  • Incorporate fresh fruits and whole grains for a balanced meal.
  • Prepare ingredients the night before for a quick morning routine.
  • Use natural sweeteners like honey or dates instead of refined sugar.
  • Hydrate well with a glass of water or a healthy smoothie.
