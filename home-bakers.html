Baking Tips

  • Use halal-certified baking ingredients such as gelatin, vanilla extract, and dairy products.
  • Ensure all utensils and baking trays are free from non-halal residues.
  • Opt for natural sweeteners like honey or dates for a healthier touch.
  • Experiment with halal-friendly flavors like saffron, cardamom, and rose water.
