Drink Tips

  • Use fresh fruits and natural sweeteners for healthier drinks.
  • Stay hydrated by incorporating herbal teas and infused water.
  • Blend smoothies with yogurt or plant-based milk for a creamy texture.
  • Avoid excessive sugar by using honey, dates, or agave syrup.
  • Experiment with spices like cinnamon or ginger for a flavorful twist.
