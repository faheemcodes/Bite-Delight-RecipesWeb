Lunch Tips

  • Include a mix of lean protein, fiber, and healthy fats for sustained energy.
  • Opt for whole grains like brown rice or whole wheat bread.
  • Pack a homemade lunch to control ingredients and portion sizes.
  • Eat a variety of colorful vegetables to get essential vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid heavy, greasy foods to prevent sluggishness in the afternoon.
