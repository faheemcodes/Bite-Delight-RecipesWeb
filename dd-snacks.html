Snack Tips

  • Choose healthy snacks with protein and fiber to keep you full longer.
  • Prepare homemade snacks to avoid unhealthy preservatives.
  • Pair snacks with a healthy dip like hummus or yogurt for extra flavor.
  • Portion your snacks to avoid overeating.
  • Try air-fried or baked alternatives instead of deep-fried snacks.
