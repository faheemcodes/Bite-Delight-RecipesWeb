BBQ Cooking Tips

  • Marinate meats for at least a few hours to enhance flavors.
  • Use a charcoal grill for authentic smoky taste.
  • Let meat rest after grilling to keep it juicy.
  • Experiment with different wood chips for unique smoke flavors.
